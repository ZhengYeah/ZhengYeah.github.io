      Ph.D. Dissertation “Local Differential Privacy: Refined Mechanism Design and Utility Analysis”  Slides

      One central direction in theoretical LDP research is the design of mechanisms that achieve improved data utility under the same privacy guarantees. This dissertation focuses on this central direction, advancing both the design of LDP mechanisms and the analysis of utility under LDP constraints. Technically, it introduces correlated perturbation into the LDP framework, establishes the optimality of piecewise-based mechanisms, and takes a first step toward theoretically quantifying classifier utility under LDP-perturbed inputs.

      硕士学位论文《多路径方法在神经网络验证中的研究与应用》- 演示文稿

      本文关注神经网络验证中的界限传播方法精度问题。围绕该问题，论文提出界限传播路径的概念， 将多种界限传播方法扩展为对应的多路径界限传播方法，并在 PyTorch 框架上实现并行化， 开发了高效且易用的鲁棒性验证工具。
