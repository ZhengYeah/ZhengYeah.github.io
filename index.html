MpBP: verifying robustness of neural networks with multi-path bound propagation
Ye Zheng, Jiaxiang Liu, Xiaomu Shi
Proceedings of the 30th ACM Joint European Software Engineering Conference and Symposium on the Foundations of Software Engineering (ESEC/FSE Demonstration), 2022
[Code]    [Slides]

MpBP (Multipath Bound Propagation) extends classical bound propagation methods with multiple propagation paths. Multiple propagation paths provides multiple pairs of bounds for a DNN, enabling tighter bounds. MpBP is based on the widely-used PyTorch machine learning framework, and paths are parallelized to provide efficient verification on GPUs.

Multi-path Back-propagation Method for Neural Network Verification (Translated into English)
Ye Zheng, Xiaomu Shi, Jiaxiang Liu
International Journal of Software and Informatics (IJSI), 2022
[Code]    [Slides]

This paper proposes the concept of back-propagation path for back-propagation methods. Under this concept, the existing back-propagation methods can be regarded as only using a single back-propagation path, which is a special case of the proposed concept. The verification accuracy of back-propagation methods can be improved significantly by using multiple back-propagation paths.
